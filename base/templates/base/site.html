Site Description

Who are the users of your site

Any human who has a list of things to accomplish for a day can be the user of this site.


What is it that you want your users to get out of using the site

The users should be able to accomplish their day to day activity by checking off the items they list using this web-app


What problem are you trying to solve for the users of the site?

I am trying to make sure users remain on track of their schedule and achieve their goal without missing any tasks. Essentially to easen the process of daily user activities.


What are the actions that you want the users to take once they have used your site

This site was created to give the students of Web Programming Class an example of what I expect in a final project.
